## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will I lose my data when changing language?
**A:** No, all your data is safe. Only the language of the text changes.

### Q: Do I need internet to change language?
**A:** No, language switching works offline.

### Q: Can I change language anytime?
**A:** Yes, you can change language as many times as you want.

### Q: Will my login be affected?
**A:** No, you'll remain logged in after changing language.

### Q: What if I select the wrong language?
**A:** Just go back to Profile and change it again. The button will be in the new language.

### Q: Does language change affect my account?
**A:** No, it only changes how text is displayed. Your account settings remain the same.
